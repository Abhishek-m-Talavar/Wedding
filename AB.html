About Us

Dandelion pixel films

At DPFilms (Dandelion pixel films ), as a freelancers we bring over 10 years of experience in professional photography and videography services, capturing life’s most extraordinary moments with creativity and precision. Our team of skilled photographers and visual artists is dedicated to delivering high-quality imagery that tells compelling stories—whether for personal memories, brands, short movies or creative projects.

We specialize in portrait, wedding, fashion, commercial, event, real estate videos, corporate and product photography & videography, offering both on-location and studio shoots. Our state-of-the-art studio is fully equipped with the photography & videography gear, lighting, and backdrops to ensure every shot is a masterpiece.

At DPFilms, we don’t just take pictures—we craft timeless visuals that leave a lasting impact. Whether you're looking for stunning portraits, cinematic wedding coverage, professional branding photos and cinematic visuals, or creative editorial photography, we’re here to turn your vision into reality.
